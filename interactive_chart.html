NY State Scholarship By The Numbers

2009 — 2016





Since 2013 the total number of students receiving state scholarships has increased.
The largest increased was recorded between 2014 and 2015 at 8.1%.
The highest total number of students though remains 2009 and in the
last seven years, this number has surpassed 23,000 only once.






Since 2013 the total amount of money available in NY state scholarships
has increased at an average of 10.7% per year. The total amount of
scholarship funding crossed 40K for the first time in 2016.






The average dollar amount received per student in scholarships has
continuously increased since 2010. It’s highest increase was
recorded between 2010 and 2011 at 10.5%. In 2016 this amount
crossed $2000 per student for the first time since 2009.
